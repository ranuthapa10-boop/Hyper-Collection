Frequently Asked

How long does delivery take? ▾

Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days across India. Express (1–2 days) and Same Day options are also available at checkout for select cities.

What is the return policy? ▾

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed with original tags. Sale items are final. Initiate a return from your account dashboard.

How do I find my size? ▾

Each product page includes a detailed size guide. We recommend measuring your chest, waist, and hips and comparing with our chart for the best fit.

Do you offer gift wrapping? ▾

Yes! Premium gift wrapping is available at checkout for ₹199. Includes a handwritten card and signature Hyper tissue paper.

Are there wholesale options? ▾

Yes, we have a wholesale program for boutiques and multi-brand retailers. Contact us at wholesale@hyperfashion.in for details.
